# B.Sc Computer Science Student Portfolio Website Plan

This plan is for building a premium, modern portfolio website tailored for a B.Sc Computer Science student. It will feature a sleek developer aesthetic (dark theme, monospace typography, clean grids, and neon accents).

## Proposed Sections
1. **Hero Section**: 
   * Name, Title (B.Sc Computer Science Student)
   * Short tagline & links to GitHub, LinkedIn, Resume
2. **About Me**: 
   * Introduction, academic background, key areas of interest (Software Engineering, Algorithms, Web Development)
3. **Tech Stack & Skills**: 
   * Categories: Languages (C++, Python, JavaScript), Frontend, Backend, Databases
4. **Projects**: 
   * Showcase major projects (e.g. your **Rain Affair Ticket Booking Site**) with screenshots and details
5. **Education & Certifications**: 
   * B.Sc in Computer Science (ongoing/completed), college details, coursework
6. **Contact**: 
   * Clean form + quick links to WhatsApp, email, and social profiles
